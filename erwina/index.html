Erwina iOS App

Designed by Vasili Savitski

I focused on all major screens in catalog: categories, brands, and products.

I used some nice features that will make your clients' shopping experience better.

I wanted to design unique look for your e-commerce up, but still keep it easy to incorporate. That's why you can use all product images as they are (no isolating required). Only 3 images for categories are to be designed.

Categories

Three categories are supported with special images. For now I used dummy images. I will incorporate products you want as soon as you provide me with their images.

Brands

After selecting a category users will be proposed to choose a brand. Every brand tile contains logo. I can also include brand's title, or the amount of products, if needed.

Products

Product card consists of a photo (no need to adjust images in order to use them), a title, a description (optional), a price tag. Tap a product to see its details or add it to the cart.

Product

Customers will have an option to add 1 item to the cart in one tap.

Swipe up and down to change the amount of products.

Tap outside the cart to get back to Products without adding anything to the cart.

Thanks for watching! Looking for your feedback so I can make some tweaks.
