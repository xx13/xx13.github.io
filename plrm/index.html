Pleroma, Concept

Last update: April 13, 2019.

Key Decisions

When I looked for the right approach, I kept the following things in mind:

  • Move away from a twitter-like outdated layout. We can take advantage of larger screens to present more content.
  • Introduce different content types. Current version works with text entries only, but videos (other types might be added as well) will be introduced.
  • Focus on Public Timeline to make exploring things easier and involve more users into communication. Though private servers is still a part of the platform.
  • Make personal profiles more personalized so they can act like a cool personal activity hub that people would love to share.
  • Landing page may be more friendly and focused on an average user. But that's a totally different story :)
